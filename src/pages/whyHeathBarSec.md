# Why I'm Starting HeathBarSec

HeathBarSec is where I'll publish practical research on cloud security, AI for defenders, and detection engineering.

The goal is simple: turn real attack techniques into useful detection logic, prevention guidance, and technical notes that practitioners can actually use.

This blog will not only be used to publish research and detections on the ever evolving Cloud environments, but also a learning opportunity for me to continue my own growth and passion for the field. 

A way for me to give back if you will.
